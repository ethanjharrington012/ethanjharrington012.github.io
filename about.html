My story

I started Simplomation to help small Maine trades spend less time chasing paperwork and more time doing the work they love. Quiet, practical automation — built on the tools you already use.

Photo: Ethan Harrington

I grew up building things — bands, side projects, little businesses. When I began helping friends who run trades companies, the pattern was obvious: quotes and follow-ups took too long, and great jobs slipped through the cracks.

My approach is simple: connect your existing tools (Google Calendar, Gmail, QuickBooks, your website) so routine steps happen automatically. No new software to learn.

Today, Simplomation helps arborists, landscapers, and contractors across Maine move faster: instant estimates, hands-free follow-ups, same-day invoicing with photos — the unglamorous stuff that keeps revenue steady.

If that sounds helpful, I’d love to hear about your workflow.
